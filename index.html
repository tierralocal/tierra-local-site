Tierra Local helps farms, food businesses, and community partners design, fund, operate, and strengthen projects that increase access to fresh, healthy local food.

We support practical local food work through grant writing, project design, operations support, facilitation, and assessment and evaluation.

What We Do

  • Grant writing and funding strategy
  • Project design and implementation planning
  • Operations support for growing programs and partnerships
  • Facilitation for cross-sector groups and collaborative processes
  • Assessment and evaluation to strengthen impact

Who We Work With

  • Farms and food producers
  • Food businesses and local buyers
  • Institutions and organizations increasing access to healthy local food
  • Community and policy partners building stronger local food systems

Why Tierra Local

Tierra Local brings together grounded strategy, strong facilitation, and practical operations thinking to help good local food ideas become real, workable projects.
