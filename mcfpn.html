Keep These In Mind
Questions to ask as you review the data
Check Yourself
What preconceived notions or perspectives do I bring into this analysis? Am I looking to confirm something I already believe?
Check the Data
Is correlation being confused with causation? Does there appear to be data missing that would help form a more complete picture?
Check the Source
Who designed the data collection and what was their motivation? Who was included vs. excluded? When was it collected?
Check the Impact
How is this data going to inform decision-making? What questions does this raise? What feels left unanswered?
